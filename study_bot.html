Features


To-do List Study Timer

To-do List



&add task:

Add a task to your to-do list.

Example: &add finish my psychology assignment

&get:


View what's on your to-do list


&wc [number]:

Cross out the task by its number, meaning that you completed the task

If you have two tasks on your to-do list, use $wc 2 to cross out the last task

&clear:


Empty your to-do list.


Study Timer



&study [minutes]:

Set a timer of [minutes] to study.

Example: Study 50 minutes: &study 50

&timer:


View the time left on your timer

&stop:


Stop the timer.
